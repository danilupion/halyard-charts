Get the application URL by running:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "common.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.service.port }}:{{ .Values.service.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
  Then visit: http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.port }}

First boot runs database migrations and seeds the app store, so the pod may
take a couple of minutes to become ready (this is covered by the startupProbe).

On first access, create your admin account at /auth/signup, then disable
public registration if desired.

NOTE: Ensure PostgreSQL is reachable and the target database exists before
deploying. The secret referenced by `database.existingSecret` must contain
both DATABASE_URL and DATABASE_DIRECT_URL (identical when no pooler is used).
